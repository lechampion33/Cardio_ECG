Cardiovascular Disease Detection

Welcome to our cutting-edge Cardiovascular Disease Detection system. We utilize advanced machine learning and deep learning techniques to analyze ECG images and provide accurate predictions of cardiovascular diseases.

Our Approach:

  • Image Preprocessing: We enhance ECG images for optimal analysis.
  • Signal Extraction: Advanced algorithms extract key features from ECG signals.
  • Dimensionality Reduction: We focus on the most relevant data for accurate diagnosis.
  • Disease Classification: Our AI models classify potential cardiovascular conditions.

Our system aims to assist healthcare professionals in early detection and diagnosis of cardiovascular diseases, potentially saving lives through timely interventions. By leveraging state-of-the-art technology, we strive to improve the accuracy and efficiency of cardiovascular disease detection.

The process begins with uploading ECG images, which are then processed through our sophisticated algorithms. These algorithms analyze the intricate patterns within the ECG signals, identifying potential anomalies that may indicate cardiovascular issues.
